Welcome to our Visual Search Engine

This project explores the CelebA dataset, containing over 200,000 celebrity images. The concept is simple: select physical attributes (e.g., Smiling, Eyeglasses, Hat...) and choose the number of images to retrieve (up to 50). Our Artificial Intelligence model will instantly find the most corresponding faces in the database.

! DISCLAIMER !

  • Model Imperfections: Our AI is not perfect and can make mistakes. Factors such as lighting, image quality, or the angle of the face can influence detection.
  • Data Subjectivity: The original labels provided by CelebA were annotated by humans. An image might visually appear to match your query but display "Match: 0/4" simply because the original human annotator did not label it that way. Traits like "Rosy Cheeks" vs "Makeup", or "Black Hair" vs "Brown Hair" can be very subjective.
  • Conflicting Queries: If you request contradictory attributes (e.g., "Black Hair" AND "Blond Hair", or "Mustache" AND "Wearing Lipstick"), the results may look strange or inaccurate, as such combinations rarely exist in the dataset.
